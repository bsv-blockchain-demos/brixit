Account

Delete My Account

This action is permanent. Deleting your account removes your personal information and cannot be undone.

What gets deleted

  • Your display name and email address
  • Your wallet identity key association
  • Your account profile and points

What is retained

Anonymised measurement submissions may be retained as part of the public scientific dataset, as described in our Privacy Policy.

How to request deletion

Account deletion is currently handled manually. To request deletion of your account:

  1. Email privacy@brix.earth from the address associated with your account, or include your wallet identity key in the request.
  2. Use the subject line: Account deletion request.
  3. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm by email.

Note: A self-service deletion feature will be added to the app in a future release.
