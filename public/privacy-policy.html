Privacy Policy

This is a placeholder document. A full privacy policy will be published before the public launch of BRIX.

1. Who we are

BRIX is a citizen science platform for collecting and visualising BRIX (sugar content / bionutrient) data in agriculture. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. What data we collect

When you create an account using a BSV wallet, we store your wallet identity key (a public key), an optional display name, and an optional email address. We also store BRIX measurement submissions you make, including the crop, brand, location, and score.

3. How we use your data

Your data is used to display leaderboards, aggregate bionutrient statistics, and attribute submissions to your account. We do not sell personal data. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Data retention and deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time via the Delete My Account page. Anonymised measurement data may be retained for scientific purposes after account deletion.

5. Cookies and tracking

We use a single HttpOnly session cookie for authentication. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or analytics that share data with advertisers.
