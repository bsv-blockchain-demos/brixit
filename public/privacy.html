Data We Collect

We do not maintain user accounts and do not collect and store personal data, such as your name or internet protocol ("IP") address. When you interact with the Services, we collect only:

The app does not collect or transmit your browsing history, cookies, web beacons, or other tracking data to us. The app may store certain information locally on your device solely to provide core functionality. This information remains on your device and is not communicated to us.

We may share or disclose the data we collect:

  • To comply with our legal obligations. We may share your data in the course of litigation, regulatory proceedings, compliance measures, and when compelled by subpoena, court order, or other legal procedure.
